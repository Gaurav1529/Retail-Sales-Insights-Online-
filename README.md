# Online Retail Sales Analysis Dashboard (Microsoft Excel)

> An end-to-end data analytics project built in **Microsoft Excel** using an Online Retail dataset. The project demonstrates the complete analytics workflow, including data cleaning, Excel formulas, Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, interactive dashboard creation, and business insight generation.

---

# Project Overview

This project analyzes historical online retail sales data to understand customer purchasing behavior, sales performance, product trends, and geographical distribution.

The analysis was completed entirely in **Microsoft Excel** without using Power Query. The workflow includes data cleaning, summary table creation, Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, Timeline filters, an interactive dashboard, and written business insights.

The objective is to transform raw transactional data into meaningful business information that supports data-driven decision making.

---

# Business Objective

The primary objectives of this analysis are to:

- Analyze overall sales performance
- Identify high-performing products
- Understand customer purchasing patterns
- Compare domestic and international sales
- Evaluate country-wise revenue contribution
- Analyze invoice trends
- Discover monthly sales seasonality
- Present findings through an interactive Excel dashboard
- Provide actionable business recommendations

---

# Dataset Description

The project uses an **Online Retail** transactional dataset containing invoice-level sales records.

Additional calculated columns were created during the cleaning process for analysis purposes.

# Key Business Insights

## 1. Strong Overall Revenue

The business generated approximately **$10.6 million** in total revenue across **19,960 invoices**, indicating a high volume of completed transactions.

---

## 2. Domestic Market Dominates Sales

- Domestic Sales: **90%**
- International Sales: **10%**

Most revenue comes from domestic customers, suggesting a strong home market presence while also indicating potential opportunities for international expansion.

---

## 3. Seasonal Sales Pattern

Sales consistently increase during:

- September
- October
- November

These months represent the strongest sales period of the year, likely influenced by seasonal shopping activity.

---

## 4. Top Revenue-Generating Products

The following products are the highest contributors to revenue:

- DOTCOM POSTAGE
- REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER
- PAPER CRAFT, LITTLE BIRDIE

Together, these three products contribute approximately **47% of total sales**, making them the primary revenue drivers.

---

## 5. Average Invoice Value

The average invoice value is approximately:

**$533.17**

This metric helps estimate the average customer purchase amount per transaction.

---

## 6. Global Customer Reach

The business serves customers across **38 countries**, demonstrating an international customer base despite domestic sales contributing the majority of revenue.

---

# Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations can be considered:

- Maintain sufficient inventory before the September–November peak sales period.
- Continue promoting the highest-performing products, as they contribute a significant share of total revenue.
- Explore opportunities to increase international sales through targeted marketing and distribution strategies.
- Monitor average invoice value and encourage higher-value purchases using product bundles or promotional offers.
- Continue tracking country-wise performance to identify emerging markets and sales opportunities.

---

# Important Note

The sales data for **December 2011** may be incomplete. Therefore, the observed decline in December sales should **not** be interpreted as a confirmed reduction in business performance without verifying data completeness.
